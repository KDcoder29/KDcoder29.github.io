Game rules
• Computer is randomly holding fingers between 0 - 5

• You have to choose the option between 0 - 5

• If your answer matches with computer then you will get a point and if it does not match, you can try again.

• You can check your luck percentage by clicking the "View my Luck %" button


• The luck percentage indicates the accuracy of the player's guesses compared to the computer's hidden number.
